### About Chris Lynch ###

Chris is an award winning writer based in Cardiff, UK.

Chris wrote [The Dark](http://www.thedarkcomicbook.com) for [AAM/Markosia](http://www.markosia.com), and is the 
co-creator of the seminal UK horror anthology "[Monkeys with Machineguns](http://www.monkeyswithmachineguns.com)".

Chris also edited the Red Cross Charity Anthology [Hammer of Time](http://www.lulu.com/spotlight/mwm).

Chris has written for a number of UK and US publications; including work for Arcana, 2026 Books, Accent UK, Something Wicked, The Sorrow, and Insomnia Publications.

Chris is CTO of [eCommerce Centric](http://www.ecommercecentric.co.uk), who own and run such brands as [Android Tablet store Technology Centric](http://www.technologycentric.co.uk). 
Chris created the [engine4 PHP Framework](http://www.engine4.net) as part of this work with [UK web development company Gravit-e Centric](http://www.gravit-e.co.uk) (of which he is also CTO)
and released the code as open source in 2012.
Chris blogs about technology, the internet, and eBusiness both here and for both eCommerce Centric and Gravit-e Centric. Chris has also been published by Linux.com.

All views and opinions on this website are Chris' own, and not those of his businesses, employers, clients, or publishers.
